## MM-T1295: Do not bounce the dock icon — macOS ONLY

---

**Step 1**

Navigate to settings/preferences:\
**Mac ONLY**: Mattermost ➜ Preferences (**⌘+,**)

1. Unselect "Bounce the Dock icon"
2. Receive an unread message

**Expected**

- Dock icon doesn't bounce
