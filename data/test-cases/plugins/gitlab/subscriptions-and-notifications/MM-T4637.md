**Step 1**

> <article>1. From the GitLab UI, select <em>Pipelines </em>under <em>CI/CD</em><br />2. Add a pipeline that will be triggered on each commit (the pipeline does not need top succeed or be functional)<br />3. In GitLab add a subscription to a channel that includes the `pipeline` event<br />4. In the GitLab project make a commit that will trigger the pipeline<br />5.
