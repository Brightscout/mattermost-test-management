### Steps

<hr/>

**Step 1**

> <article>Should display share button when mobile file download is disabled<br>--------------------<br>1. As admin, go to <strong>Site Configuration &gt;&nbsp;</strong><strong>File Sharing and Downloads&nbsp;</strong>and set,<ul><li><strong>Allow File Sharing = true</strong></li><li><strong>Allow File Downloads on Mobile = true</strong></li></ul>2. As user, login to app and go to a channel<br>3. Post a file, image, or captured photo/video<br>4. Tap on the posted file, image, or captured photo/video<br>5. Tap on share button</article>
