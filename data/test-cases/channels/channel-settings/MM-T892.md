## MM-T892: Leave a channel while multiple other tabs are open to different channels

### Details

### Steps

<hr/>

**Step 1**

> <article><ol><li>Log on to a server on a browser and view a channel you want to leave</li><li>Open multiple additional tabs, each viewing a different channel on that server</li><li>Go back to the first tab, click channel name drop-down and select `Leave Channel`</li></ol></article>

**Expected**

> <article><ul><li>Only the tab viewing the channel you left is redirected to whatever channel you had previously been viewing on that tab.</li><li>Other tabs do not redirect; they continue to display the channel you had been viewing on each tab (the channels you did not leave)</li></ul></article>
