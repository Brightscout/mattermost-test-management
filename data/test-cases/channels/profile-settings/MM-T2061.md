## MM-T2061: Nickname - Cancel edit

---

**Step 1**

Nickname - Cancel edit\
—————————————\
1\. On an account that has a nickname set:\
2\. Go to User menu > Profile > Profile Settings > Nickname > Edit\
3\. Enter a new value\
4\. Click Cancel

**Expected**

Nickname field reverts to previous value.
