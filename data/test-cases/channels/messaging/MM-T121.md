### Steps

<hr/>

**Step 1**

> <article>Escape should not close modal when an autocomplete drop down is in use<br />--------------------<br />1. Post a message<br />2. Hit the up arrow to open the &quot;edit modal&quot;<br />3. In the modal type @ and note the user autocomplete is visible<br />4. Press the escape key, observe autocomplete closes<br />5. In the modal type ~ and note the channel autocomplete is visible<br />6. Press the escape key<br />7. In the modal click the emoji picker icon<br />8. Press the escape key</article>

**Expected**

> <article>On 4. the user auto-complete drop down closes but the edit modal remains open<br /><br />On 6. the chanel auto-complete drop down closes but the edit modal remains open<br /><br />On 8. the emoji picker closes but the edit modal remains open</article>
