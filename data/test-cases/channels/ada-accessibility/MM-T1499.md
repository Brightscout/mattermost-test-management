### Steps

<hr/>

**Step 1**

> <article>Screen reader should not switch to virtual cursor mode while navigating with CTRL+F6/F6, arrow keys or TAB, unless virtual cursor mode is toggled on/off by the user<br>–––––––––––––––––––––––––<ol><li>Open JAWS and toggle virtual PC cursor off with INS+Z</li><li>Use CTRL+F6/F6 to navigate and loop through all app regions</li><li>In each regions, use up/down arrows or TAB and ensure green highlighting is not seen</li></ol></article>

**Expected**

> <article><ul><li>When navigating between different app regions and while navigating within each region, green highlighting should not be seen.</li><li>Screen reader stays in "form mode" and does not switch over to virtual cursor mode<br><strong>Tip</strong>: You can check whether virtual cursor mode is on/off by pressing "Insert+Z" on your keyboard (this toggles virtual cursor mode on/off</li></ul></article>
