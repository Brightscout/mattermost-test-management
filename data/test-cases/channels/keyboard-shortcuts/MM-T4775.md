## MM-T4775: CTRL/CMD+SHIFT+I - Toggle Channel Info RHS

---

**Step 1**

1. Visit a channel
2. Ensure the RHS is not visible
3. Press Ctrl+Shift+I (Windows) or ⌘+⇧+I (macOS)
4. Press the keys again

**Expected**

After 3:

- The RHS should open

After 4:

- The RHS should close
