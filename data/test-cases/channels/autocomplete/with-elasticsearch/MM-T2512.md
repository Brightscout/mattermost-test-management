### Steps

<hr/>

**Step 1**

> <article><ol><li>Log in as a UserA using email/password credentials</li><li>In an incognito window, log in as UserB</li><li>In a public channel common to both users, UserB "@mention" UserA</li><li>UserA change their username to include a dot, dash or underscore in the username</li><li>In the same public channel common to both users, UserB "@mention" UserA</li></ol></article>

**Expected**

> <article>After step 3, userA is returned in the autocomplete suggestions.<br>After step 5, userA is returned in the autocomplete suggestions showing updated username.</article>
