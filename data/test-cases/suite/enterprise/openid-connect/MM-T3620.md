## MM-T3620: Set to Google OpenId

---

**Step 1**

1. Login as Admin.
2. Go to System Console.
3. Click **OpenID connect **
4. Select **Google Apps **from the Drop Down List.
5. Input in **Client ID**.
6. Input in **Client Secret**
7. Click **Save** button
8. Logout
9. Go to Login Page

**Test Data**
