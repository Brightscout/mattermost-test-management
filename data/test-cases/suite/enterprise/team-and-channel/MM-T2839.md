### Steps

<hr/>

**Step 1**

> <article>1. Login to System Console as sysadmin user and visit Teams Page. <br />2. Click on any team which is not LDAP Group Synced.<br />3. Click on Add Members and check if only list of members who are not there in the team are shown. <br />4. Select few users and then add them and check if those users are added in the top of the table with a NEW flag indicating that the users will be added upon save. <br />5. Click on Save button. Reload the page and check that newly added users are displayed in the Team Members block.</article>

**Expected**

> <article>3. Only list of members who are not added in the team should be shown in the Add Members dialog. <br />4. Newly added users should be shown in the top of the table with a NEW flag. <br />5. Newly added users should be shown in the Team Members block when page is reloaded. NEW flag should not be shown now.</article>
