## MM-T2840: Add Members outside allowed email domains

**Objective**

> <article>Add Members outside allowed email domains</article>

**Precondition**

> <article>None</article>

### Steps

<hr/>

**Step 1**

> <article>1. Login to System Console as sysadmin user and visit Teams Page. <br />2. Click on any team which is not LDAP Group Synced<br />3. Enable the option &quot;Only specific email domains can join this team&quot; and specify the domain as &quot;mattermost.org&quot; and save. <br />4. Click on the same Team again and then click on Add Members. <br />5. Select a user whose email does not belongs to &quot;mattermost.org&quot; domain and try to save.</article>

**Expected**

> <article>An error should be displayed if the sysadmin tries to add a member outside the whitelisted list of email domains.</article>
