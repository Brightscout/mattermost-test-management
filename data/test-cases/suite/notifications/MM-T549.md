**Step 1**

> <article>Words that trigger mentions - Custom word<br>–––––––––––––––––––––––––<ol><li>Click the hamburger main menu</li><li>Select "Account Settings" then select "Notifications"</li><li>Click "Edit" to the right of "Words that trigger mentions"</li><li>Select "Other non-case sensitive words, separated by commas"</li><li>Enter the word "<strong>asparagus</strong>"</li><li>Click "Save"</li><li>Use another account to post a message containing the word `Asparagus` with a capital `A`</li></ol></article>

**Expected**

> <article><ul><li>You receive a notification, channel is bolded in LHS and shows a mention jewel</li><li>The word 'asparagus' is highlighted</li></ul></article>
