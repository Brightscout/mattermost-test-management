## MM-T4668: User Groups Modal-Join Group

---

**Step 1**

1. Login as UserA
2. Create custom group GroupA
3. Click Product Switcher Menu 
4. Click User Groups option
5. Select GroupA
6. Click 3 dots menu icon next to Add People button
7. Click Join-Group

**Expected**

7\. Group should be joined
