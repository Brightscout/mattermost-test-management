## MM-T4933: Allows users to remove themselves from a custom role

---

**Step 1**

1\. Login as SysAdmin\
2\. Go to Sytem Console > User Management > System Role > Custom Group Manager\
3\. Click Add People\
4\. Select SysAdmin\
5\. Click save\
6\. Remove SysAdmin

**Expected**

5\. SysAdmin should be added\
6\. SysAdmins should be removed,
