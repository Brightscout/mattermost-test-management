### Steps

<hr/>

**Step 1**

> <article><ol><li>Ensure SAML setup is done correctly on an instance</li><li>Navigate to System Console &gt; SAML 2.0 and set Guest Attribute to "isGuest=true"</li><li>Login as a SAML user who has this attribute "isGuest=true" configured in the SAML Server</li><li>Now login as sysadmin and remove the Guest Attribute and check</li></ol></article>

**Expected**

> <article><ul><li>Existing guest users should not be promoted to members.</li><li>Any new user who tries to login with isGuest=true will no longer be considered a Guest user and instead should be considered a regular member</li></ul></article>
