### Steps

<hr/>

**Step 1**

> <article>Invalidate Pending Email Invites<br>–––––––––––––––––––––––––<ol><li>Login to the application and click on Invite People and invite a guest.</li><li>Now login as a Sysadmin and navigate to System Console -&gt; Sign Up Page.</li><li>Click on Invalidate Pending Email Invites.</li><li>Now click on the link sent to the invited guest user and try to complete the signup process</li></ol></article>

**Expected**

> <article><ul><li>All the existing email invites should be invalidated even for Guest users.</li><li>User should not be able to complete the signup process and create an account with a link which was expired by Sysadmin.</li></ul></article>
