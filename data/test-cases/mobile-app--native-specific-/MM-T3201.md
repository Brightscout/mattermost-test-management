### Steps

<hr/>

**Step 1**

> <article><ol><li>From the channels list, tap on the "+" displayed to the right of "CHANNELS"</li><li>Tap on "Create" bottomsheet option (Public Channel type is pre-selected in the Create screen, otherwise choose Public Channel)</li><li>Type in a name, observing that the `Create` button is not tappable until at least 2 characters have been entered</li><li>Type in a purpose that has multiple returns (new lines)</li><li>Type in a header that is an emoji (such as ` :taco: `)</li><li>Tap "Create" in the top right of the screen</li></ol></article>

**Expected**

> <article>On step 1, a list of existing channels should be displayed.<br />On step 2, a screen enabling you to name your channel should be displayed.<br />On step 6,<br /><ul><li>The new channel should be created and you should be redirected to the new channel</li><li>You can tap the channel name to view and verify channel purpose and header</li></ul><br /></article>
