<hr/>

**Step 1**

> <article>Archive public or private channel<br>–––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––<br><ol><li>Tap the channel header</li><li>Tap on 'archive channel'</li><li>Tap on "yes"</li></ol></article>

**Expected**

> <article>After #2,<br><ul><li>A message is displayed on screen asking whether or not you want to archive the channel.</li></ul>After #3,<br><ul><li>The channel is archived and you are redirected to the last channel you were viewing</li><li>If you had tapped on "no", the confirmation should just close and your view remains in channel info<br><br></li></ul><strong>Note</strong>: If "Allow users to view archived channels" is set to "True" - view will remain in archived channel view of the channel you've just archived<br><br></article>
