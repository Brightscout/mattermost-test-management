### Steps

<hr/>

**Step 1**

> <article>Should be able to switch between teams<br>--------------------<br>1. Login to server where you belong to more than one team<br>2. Open main sidebar, tap on <strong>switch teams button</strong> (or swipe right)<br>3. Tap on <strong>first team</strong><br>4. Repeat step 2 and tap on <strong>second team</strong> (different from current team)<br>5. Repeat step 2</article>

**Expected**

> <article>After #1,<ul><li>Verify <strong>first team channel</strong> is displayed</li></ul><br>After #2,<ul><li>Verify <strong>team sidebar</strong> is opened</li><li>Verify first team is current (checked)</li></ul><br>After #3,<ul><li>Verify team sidebar is dismissed and <strong>first team channel&nbsp;</strong>is displayed</li></ul><br>After #4,<ul><li>Verify team sidebar is dismissed and <strong>second team channel&nbsp;</strong>is displayed</li></ul><br>After #5,<ul><li>Verify first team is <strong>not</strong> current (not checked)</li><li>Verify second team <strong>is</strong> current (checked)</li></ul><br><br>Note: Direct / Group message list should be the same across teams on the same server.<ul><li>A separate test case will be created for the verifying DMs and GMs across teams to include more detailed steps for automation</li></ul></article>
