### Steps

<hr/>

**Step 1**

> <article>Should be able to change setting to Immediately<br>————————————————————————————<ol><li>Tap the three-dot menu in the top right of your screen</li><li>Tap "Settings", then "Notifications"</li><li>Tap "Email"</li><li>Change the setting to Immediately</li><li>Exit settings back to channel view, then open Email settings again</li></ol></article>

**Test Data**

> <article>Set user status to either away or offline in order to allow to receive email notifications</article>

**Expected**

> <article><ul><li>The setting is saved to Immediately</li><li>Email is received on a mention if test user has email address you can access</li></ul></article>

<hr/>

**Step 2**

> <article>Should be able to change setting to Never<br>————————————————————————————<ol><li>Tap the three-dot menu in the top right of your screen</li><li>Tap "Settings", then "Notifications"</li><li>Tap "Email"</li><li>Change the setting to Never</li><li>Exit settings back to channel view, then open Email settings again</li></ol></article>

**Expected**

> <article><ul><li>The setting is saved to Never</li><li>Email is never received on a mention</li></ul></article>
