## MM-T4295: Pressing Esc on a URL property does not cause a crash

---

**Step 1**

1. Open a card with a URL property
2. Click on the url property
3. (Optional) Enter or paste in a URL
4. Press Esc

**Expected**

No error. Esc should accept the input.
