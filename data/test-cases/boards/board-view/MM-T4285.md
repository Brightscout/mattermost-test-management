## MM-T4285: Adding group color

---

**Step 1**

1. Add a group (see MM-T4284)
2. Click on the three dots next to "Group 1"
3. Click on "Green"

**Expected**

On step 2, dropdown opens with Hide, Delete and color options.\
On step 3, "Group 1" color changes to Green.
